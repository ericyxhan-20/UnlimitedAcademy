SAT 1 Classes

At Gateway Academy, we have produced many students with perfect scores and students with scores in the top 5 percentile! SAT subject tests are not only a great way to show colleges your readiness in the subject content, but they are also required by most competitive colleges and by some choice of majors.

Many subjects are available to take; however, you should choose the subjects you know well and prepare for the test. We offer private tutoring year round, but classes are available in May, after the AP test, and in the summer, from June through August. We provide a thorough review of the subject matter along with practice tests and test reviews.
