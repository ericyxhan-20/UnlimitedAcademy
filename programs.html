AP Classes

Advanced Placement exams test students on introductory college-level material. These exams present the perfect opportunity to both demonstrate your ability and willingness to go above and beyond the high school curriculum as well as earn credits that will count towards your graduation requirements wherever you end up at!

There are over 30 different subjects available to take; however, here at Unlimited Academy we encourage our students to take the time to explore areas they may be interested in. Doing so also provides you a chance to glimpse into your future should you choose to major in that subject.

We provide a thorough review of all topics and content covered by the exam along with countless practice tests and test reviews to make sure you feel more than prepared on test day!

College Counseling

Overwhelmed by the college application process? Not sure where to start with your essays? Unlimited Prep Academy's top-tier college counselors provide guidance on all aspects of the application process including picking colleges, resume writing, essay writing and editing, and filling out the application.

Your application is your life's story, and our counselors are experts in constructing an application profile that best matches your career goals and most highlights your achievements.

With many years experience helping students get into their dream college, Unlimited Prep Academy is excited to help you reach yours!
