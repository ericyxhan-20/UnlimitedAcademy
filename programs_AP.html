ADVANCED PLACEMENT

Our talented and experienced academic tutors break down the daunting Advanced Placement exam process into easily manageable assignments helping students avoid any pressure they may encounter. Our teachers guide students on assignment support, test preparation, exam-taking strategies, and more.

AP exams are an ideal way to demonstrate your college-readiness to potential universities while earning college credit at the same time! Many of our students have been able to waive as much as an entire year of general education classes from AP credits earned in high school.

For parents seeking college advice, our staff is knowledgeable on all public and private universities, both within and outside the United States. Whatever your education goals are, Unlimited Prep Academy will help you achieve your highest score!
