About Us

Hi! My name is Eric Han, Chief Director at Unlimited Prep Academy.
Throughout my experience, I have gained valuable insight into the unique challenges that students face, having gone through this process myself,
and understand how overwhelming it can be for students to navigate the complex world of standardized testing and higher education.

We believe that every student deserves a personalized approach to learning that is tailored to their unique needs, learning style, and background
and offer a range of tutoring services that are designed to help students excel in their academic pursuits and reach their full potential.

At Unlimited Prep Academy, we take great pride in the success of our students, and our goal is to provide the highest quality tutoring services
to help them achieve their academic and career aspirations. Whether you're looking to improve your standardized test scores, need help with a certain
subject or topic, or are looking for guidance on the college admissions process, we are here to help.

I invite you to join our community and experience the difference that personalized and comprehensive tutoring services can make in your academic journey!

Who We Are

Unlimited Prep Academy prides itself on its team of highly qualified tutors who hail from prestigious universities across the world.

Our college graduate collective brings together a diverse range of academic backgrounds and experiences, ensuring that we can provide personalized and comprehensive tutoring services to students from all walks of life. Our tutors are passionate about helping students achieve their academic goals and have decades of combined experience working with students.

At Unlimited Prep Academy, we are dedicated to helping students reach their full potential and achieve academic success. Our services are designed to help students excel in their academic pursuits and reach their college and career aspirations. Whether you need help with standardized testing, college essay writing, or college admissions counseling, our team of experts is here to help you every step of the way.

What We Do

Since the global COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, we have seen first-hand the effectiveness of online learning both in our students as well as in our own studies.

We sought to bring this experience to others.

Here at Unlimited Prep Academy, we challenge the idea that learning is less productive in an online format, and our proven results have demonstrated its effectiveness.

Unlimited Prep Academy prides itself in the quality of our refined programs, and nearly perfect satisfaction rate, so you can be sure to trust us!
