About Us

Hi! My name is Eric Han, Chief Director at Unlimited Prep Academy.
Throughout my experience, I have gained valuable insight into the unique challenges that students face, having gone through this process myself,
and understand how overwhelming it can be for students to navigate the complex world of standardized testing and higher education.
That's why I decided to create Unlimited Prep Academy.

As a high-schooler, I often felt nervous about the college application process, simply because I lacked the experience and knowledge necessary to effectively nevigate it. As a graduate who has now seen this process repeat every single year, I only wish that I had known then what I know now.

Here at Unlimited Prep Academy, we connect our students to a whole network of current students and alumni from the very universities they are interested in. We seek to bridge this knowledge gap and bring our experiences directly to you, and our proven results have demonstrated the effectiveness of this strategy.

I invite you to join our community and experience the difference that our network of personalized and comprehensive services can make in your academic journey!

Who We Are

Unlimited Prep Academy prides itself on its highly qualified team who hail from prestigious universities across the world.

Our college graduate collective brings together a diverse range of academic backgrounds and experiences, ensuring that we can provide personalized and comprehensive tutoring services to students from all walks of life. Our tutors are passionate about helping students achieve their academic goals and have decades of combined experience working with students.

At Unlimited Prep Academy, we are dedicated to helping students reach their full potential and achieve academic success. Our services are designed to help students excel in their academic pursuits and reach their college and career aspirations. Whether you need help with standardized testing, college essay writing, or college admissions counseling, our team of experts is here to help you every step of the way.
