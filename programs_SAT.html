SAT/ACT PROGRAM

Whether you are taking the SAT or the ACT, or even if you need help deciding which one is right for you, our teachers and staff are there to guide you ever step along the way! Despite recent buzz about the decline in requirements for standardized testing, most top-tier schools are re-instating their SAT and ACT requirements when applying. These exams are the perfect chance to show your dream school that you are serious about academics and can handle their rigorous courseload.

Unlimited Prep Academy has assembled teachers from the ranks of Princeton, Caltech, Harvard, UC Berkeley, and more to help you navigate through this important exam!
